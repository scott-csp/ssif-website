How It Works

A Simple Path to Impact Investment

We've streamlined the investment process so you can start making a difference with confidence.

1

Invest in the Fund

Contribute capital to the Somalia Solar Investment Fund. Minimum investments start at an accessible level to welcome a broad range of investors.

2

Clients Access Shariah-Compliant Financing

Your capital is extended as Shariah-compliant financing to Somali businesses, institutions, and households to purchase solar equipment and installation from ClearSky Power.

3

ClearSky Power Delivers

Our implementation partner, ClearSky Power, manages engineering, procurement, and construction of each solar installation.

4

Earn Returns & Impact

As clients repay their financing, revenue flows back to the fund and is distributed to investors. You earn competitive returns while communities across Somalia gain access to clean, affordable energy.

Investment Details

Structured for Performance & Transparency

Every aspect of the fund is designed to align investor interests with community outcomes.

📈

Target Returns

The fund targets annual returns of 6–10%, aligned with social impact investing benchmarks, generated as clients repay Shariah-compliant financing for their solar equipment and installation.

🛡

Risk Mitigation

Diversified across multiple project types (mini-grids, commercial solar, agricultural pumps) and geographies to reduce concentrated risk exposure.

📊

Quarterly Reporting

Investors receive detailed quarterly reports covering financial performance, project milestones, energy output data, and community impact metrics.

🌎

ESG Aligned

Every dollar invested directly contributes to UN Sustainable Development Goals 7 (Affordable Energy), 8 (Decent Work), and 13 (Climate Action).

🔒

Fund Structure

Structured as a closed-end fund with a 5-year term. Capital is called in tranches aligned with project deployment schedules for optimal capital efficiency.

🤝

Expert Partners

Projects are implemented by ClearSky Power, an established solar EPC firm headquartered in Hargeisa with operations across the Horn of Africa since inception.
